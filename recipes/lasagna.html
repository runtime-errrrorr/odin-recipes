Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a type of pasta, possibly one of the oldest types, made of very wide, flat sheets.

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made with layers of pasta, sauce, meat, and cheese.
The pasta is wide and flat, with wavy edges that make it ideal for layering.
The dish is traditionally made with béchamel sauce, ragù, and Parmigiano-Reggiano cheese. However, there are many different lasagna recipes.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasoning
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the instructions
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving
